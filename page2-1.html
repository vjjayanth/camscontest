TS-Epass

The Telangana government has revolutionised the process of awarding scholarships to SC/ST/BC/EBC/Minority, and PWD students. It has entrusted the responsibility of online approval of Post-matriculation Scholarships (PMS) to these students through their Electronic Payment and Application System of Scholarships, or ePASS.

Reliance UG & PG Scholarship

Reliance Foundation has announced 5000 UG and 100 PG scholarships for 2022-23 on the occasion of the 90th birth anniversary of Dhirubhai Ambani, Founder - Chairman of Reliance Industries Limited. The Foundation will also award 50,000 scholarships over the next 10 years for students pursuing higher education in India.

Amazon Future Engineer Scholarship

With the Amazon Future Engineering Scholarship, high-school students from underserved backgrounds can win scholarships worth $40,000, for four years of education in computer sciences. The award also includes a paid programming internship at Amazon. Each year, 100 students are selected for this scholarship and internship program.
